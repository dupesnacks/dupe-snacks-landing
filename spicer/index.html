About Darren Spicer

Serial Entrepreneur. Strategic Advisor. Founder's Founder.

Darren Spicer is a founder and operator with a proven track record of building, scaling, and exiting category-defining brands. His first exit—Clutch Coffee Bar to Dutch Bros—demonstrated mastery in brand building, sales strategy, and operational excellence across multi-location QSR concepts.

Since the exit, Darren has channeled his expertise into helping other founders and brands reach their potential through consulting, coaching, and strategic advising. His approach is direct, experience-based, and focused on the fundamentals that actually move the needle.
