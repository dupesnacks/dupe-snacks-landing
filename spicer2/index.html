The Path to Advising

I started as a founder because I had a belief: that category-defining brands could be built from the ground up by operators who actually cared about the product and the people.

I founded Clutch Coffee Bar with that mindset. We built it intentionally—17 locations of focused brand experience, every detail deliberate. Then we scaled it intentionally, and exited intentionally to Dutch Bros in 2024.

That exit was the beginning of something different. It taught me that the playbook works. It's not luck. It's not random. It's a specific set of decisions made consistently over time: on product, on culture, on go-to-market, on partnerships, on when to scale and when to hold.

Now I work with founders and brands who want to learn that playbook. I advise. I coach. I build. And I bring the experience of someone who's been in the trenches—not someone lecturing from theory.

Through 110 Ventures, I invest in and advise emerging brands (Big Drink Energy, Cowboy MUD, Kimmie Pops, Take 5 Oil Change) and provide strategic guidance to founders who are serious about building the next category-defining brand.
